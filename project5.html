Rising Pixel Internship

Explore my journey with this awesome Studio.

Slime Clicker

Slime clicker was developed in house at my time at rising pixel, during this project I could work hand in hand with a codeveloper and took the role of support. The core concept was that of a generic clicker game, and served as a playground for ideas surrounding the character Luna. I was implementing new features, providing feedback and took an active role during the creation of this browser game.

Discount Fusion

A blueprint for future Advertgames. During my time at Rising Pixel I was tasked with creating a playable blueprint that could be easily reused for future advertgames. The core gameloop lets players drop discounts from the top. If two discounts of the same value collide they fuse together creating a larger discount. Once the player reaches the maximum discount or if a time limit runs out the player can then choose to use the discount for a purchase or try again if they did not reach the biggest reward.

The Journey

I had the pleasure of undertaking an internship abroad with Rising Pixel, a small independent company dedicated to the creation of games, located in the city of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria in Spain. Rising Pixel is a creative studio dedicated to crafting experiences that blend games with actual needs and purpose. Their portfolio spans across video games, interactive apps and gamified solutions for businesses, with a strong focus on accessibility.

If you would like to learn more about Rising Pixel you may click here to visit their offical website.

During my six months with the company I traveled to the Canary Islands and had the opportunity to work with the team on various projects. I used this time to gain experience within the industry, learned to use new tools, strengthen my understanding of the field as a software developer and tackle a variety of new and exciting challenges. Although I was not able to speak spanish, I was able to communicate and work effectively with my colleagues as a team, explore the Island and interact with locals.

My Training

After getting myself familiar with the team and the company structure I was introduced to the game engine Construct 3, a large part of my work took place within the bounds of this tool. I was already familiar with other engines such as Unity which enabled me to pick up the pace extremely quickly. Java Script, which I was already familiar with, was the main programming language I used during my Internship. After the first week I was assigned to my first project at Rising Pixel. In the following months I worked successfully on various projects. My efforts were rewarded with ever increasing responsibilities and the trust of my colleagues.

Overall I had an amazing time at the studio and I am proud to be able to present a few of the projects I had worked on during this adventure.

Hire Rush

Early on I had the opportunity to work with an existing game at Rising Pixel. Hire rush lets the player collect hires by traveling across a game map while defending or stealing hires from other npcs. My main responsibility was to develop a local multiplayer, featuring a co-op and pvp mode. I was furthermore adding various other small features, fixing bugs and streamlining the codebase.
